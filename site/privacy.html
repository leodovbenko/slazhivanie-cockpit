Privacy Policy

Uklad AI · effective 19 July 2026

Uklad AI (“we”, “the service”, uklad.ai) is a personal AI assistant operated by Leonid Dovbenko. This policy explains what data we access when you connect a health or wellness device, and how we use it. Questions: leo@dovbenko.me.

What we access

Only with your explicit authorization (OAuth consent), and only for accounts you own:

  • Health & wellness data from Oura and/or WHOOP: recovery/readiness score, sleep, heart-rate variability, resting heart rate, heart-rate series, daily activity and strain, stress, and workout/session records.
  • Work calendar you connect (event start/end times and titles), used to find free time in your day.

We request the minimum scopes needed for the features you use. You can review and revoke access at any time in your device provider’s account settings.

How we use it

Solely to provide you personal coaching inside your own Uklad assistant — for example, to suggest a good time to train based on your recovery and free calendar windows, or to suggest rest when your body needs it, and to track a fitness goal you set yourself.

We do not sell your data, do not use it for advertising, and do not share it with third parties. Your health data is never mixed with any other person’s or client’s data.

Storage & security

Access tokens and derived daily metrics are stored encrypted at rest on our servers (EU region, AWS Stockholm) and scoped to your account. Access is limited to you and the assistant acting on your behalf.

Retention & deletion

You may disconnect Oura or WHOOP at any time. On disconnect we delete the stored access tokens and biometric data derived from that device. You may also request full deletion of your data by emailing leo@dovbenko.me.

Data source

Health data is obtained through the Oura and WHOOP APIs under your authorization and remains subject to their respective terms and privacy policies.

Changes

We may update this policy; the effective date above reflects the latest version.
