What generic AI for posts gets wrong

Four places where a post generator falls short, and what Hermes does about each.

Writes in generalitiesIt doesn't know your business, so posts come out vague and thin. Hermes takes the angle from your business memory and shows where it came from.
Can leak private dataA client name or deal detail can slip into a public post. Hermes catches it in the text and offers to anonymize before you ever see it.
Text is only half the jobYou need slides and on-brand visuals, not just words. Hermes renders the carousel from your brand template.
One channel, you need manyYou won't repost to LinkedIn, Telegram and a blog by hand. Hermes turns one angle into a version per channel and publishes it.
